Grassroots Talent Initiative

🌱 Khel Nursery

Early Sports Discovery & Foundational Skill Development

The Khel Nursery Initiative focuses on discovering and nurturing sports talent at an early age (6–12 years). Through engaging and structured physical activities, young learners develop basic athletic skills, coordination, discipline, and a lifelong passion for sports. The initiative builds a strong foundation in fitness, movement, teamwork, and sports awareness, preparing children for advanced training pathways in their chosen sport, including cricket and other disciplines.
